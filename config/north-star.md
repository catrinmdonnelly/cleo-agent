# North star

*The big picture. Where this business is going. Cleo reads this before every weekly brief, so make sure it captures the things that don't change week to week.*

## What we sell

Replace this with a one-paragraph description of what you sell, who you sell it to, and what makes it different from the obvious alternatives.

## Where we want to be in 12 months

Replace this with the specific milestone you're aiming for. Revenue number, customer count, market position, whichever is most useful to you. Be specific. "Grow the business" is too vague to be useful.

## Where we want to be in 3 years

Replace this with the bigger vision. Cleo uses this to spot moves that compound, not just moves that win this week.

## Who this is for

Replace this with a sharp picture of your ideal customer. Demographics matter less than: what they're trying to do, what they currently use, what they hate about it, where they hang out online.

## What we will not do

Replace this with the things that are off the table. Markets you won't enter, formats you won't use, audiences you won't chase. This is as important as what you will do.
